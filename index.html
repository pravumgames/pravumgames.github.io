The software and game development team

Who are we?

We're group of people that was formed by Daniel Nesov and Piotr Horak. We are newcomers to serious game development, but we all had previous experiences in the past. We're developing experimental software, and our first game: Dyingworld and expansion to it.

We're driven only by motivation.

Pravum is a place where folks with similar minds come together to make something cool and unique. We're not the evil game company: we are just individuals that want to create something amazing with others. We think that if you don't enjoy the process of creating the project, the productivity will drop, which leads to worse creativity and quality of the project.

That's why we organized our work the way, that you can switch between projects. If you're bored with gray and dystopian world of Dyingworld, you can switch to other projects.

Our team

Our team consists of highly talented artists and musicians, creative writers and designers, and experienced programmers. Each of us has a significantly different age and is from other part of the planet. Such things are not barriers for us, and we will always find a way to communicate. Together we can create quality code, unique worlds, engaging gameplays and memorable soundtracks.
