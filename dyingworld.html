What is DYINGWORLD?

Game is set in year 2025, in alternative universe, where European Union is socialist. It is called “United Countries of Socialist Europe” UCSE in short. The AI technology reaches it’s peak: which allows it to control cities and make decisions on it’s own. The actual government consists of 4 people that are called “Overseers”. Tired of this, civilians decide to rebel against the system. Before, there were several terrorist attacks, that made decision to create “RSS”, which stands for “Rapid Situation Squad”, which in case of inevitable danger at larger scale will act fast and efficient, with leaving minimum amount of clues of what happened, to avoid next attack.

RPG

The game has weapon upgrades, such as scopes, silencers and etc. The upgrading system will allow you to improve several parameters (intelligence, strength and condition) depending on exercises you make. For example, when you run, your condition and strength will slowly increase. When hacking, reading the book your intelligence will increase. When using weapons, you’ll get strength points.

Psychological Horror

To make the appropriate atmosphere of game’s world, the system of sanity will be introduced. When player kills others, gets damage, is in danger, his sanity will decrease which will lead to hallucinations.

Meet THEM

The game has 2 teams to play as: Rebels and City

REBELS

They want freedom, at all costs. Chances of winning against the city are near zero, but they will not give up. When you play as Rebel, try to find a lot of medical supplies and weapons. Since you are not ready for combat, because you were ordinary civilians, you will need to upgrade your skills and experience mental illnesses.
