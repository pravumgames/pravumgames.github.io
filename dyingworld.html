DYINGWORLD

Welcome to a new world.

Dyingworld is a first person roleplaying game, set in alternative world, where totalitarianism was spread through the rest of the world.
Cities are being monitored by a one big artificial intelligence.

Gameplay

Each team has unique tasks to accomplish, except Neutrals. Neutrals are ordinary dwellers of the city that can live and work, like in real life.

Criminals are the bad guys for Police, obviously. They can team up with Neutrals, to get advantage.
Their task is to take control over city, kill every member of Police or destroy CAI. Police will win only when every member of Criminals is arrested or neutralised.

Police is self explanatory. Their task is to prevent Criminals to sell drugs and destroying the CAI.
If there will be no alive Police members, they will be given the last opportunity to be spawned as a RSS. As RSS, they will have 10x greater advantage.
As mentioned before, Police will win if every member of Criminals is neutralised or arrested.
